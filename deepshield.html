We believe access to truth-verification technology should be universal, not gatekept behind expensive enterprise contracts.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is Castellan free to use?▾
Yes — Castellan offers a free tier with up to 20 analyses per month. Paid plans unlock unlimited scans, API access, and priority processing.
Are my uploaded files stored?▾
Never. Files are analyzed in-memory using an ephemeral processing pipeline and deleted immediately after the scan completes. We do not store, log, or share your media.
What file types are supported?▾
Images: JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, BMP. Videos: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV (up to 500 MB). Audio: MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG. URL scanning is also available for publicly hosted media.
How accurate is the detection?▾
Our MultiModal model achieves 98.6% accuracy on standard benchmarks (FaceForensics++, DFDC, WildDeepfake). Individual models range from 91–97.3% depending on media type.
Do you offer an API?▾
Yes. Our REST API is available on paid plans. You can integrate deepfake detection directly into your product, CMS, or moderation pipeline. Full docs at docs.Castellan.ai.
